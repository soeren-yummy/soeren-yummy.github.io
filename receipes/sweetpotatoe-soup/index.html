Sweetpotatoe Soup

2018-01-27
soup sweetpotatoe vegetarian chickpeas

Ingredients

  • One onion, chopped to small pieces
  • Two garlic cloves, chopped
  • 700g sweetpotatoe, chopped into 1cm chunks
  • 3-4 carrots, chopped into small discs
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • around 1.3l broth
  • 1-2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • Fresh coriander
  • Optional: 1 chili pepper

Spices

  • (White) pepper
  • Coriander
  • Cumin

Instructions

  1. Add everything except coriander and lemon juice to the crockpot
  2. Cook for 4 hours on high (or 8h on low), blend everything to a fine soup
  3. Add the lemon juice
  4. Serve with chopped coriander
