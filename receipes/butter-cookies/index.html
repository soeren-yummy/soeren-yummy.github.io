Butter Cookies

2020-12-07
christmas cookies matcha vanilla sweets

Ingredients:

  • 200g flour
  • 60-75g sugar
  • 175 butter
  • 1 egg

Optional:

  • 2 tb matcha powder
  • 1 vanilla pod

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the ingredients, knead thoroughly
  2. Put it in the fridge for about 10m
  3. Pre-heat the oven to 120°C, roll out the dough about ~5mm thick
  4. Bake for 6-8 minutes
