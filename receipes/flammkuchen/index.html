Flammkuchen / tarte flambée

2020-07-05
german traditional easy fast mushroom cheese

Servings

Produces 2 Flammkuchen

Ingredients:

  • 200g flour
  • 1 ts salt
  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 110ml water

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Knead them for 3 minutes

Variations:

  • Gratinée: with Gruyère cheese
  • Forestière: with mushrooms
  • Sweet: dessert version with apples and cinnamon, flambéed with Calvados
