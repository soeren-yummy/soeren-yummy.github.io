Kartoffelpfannkuchen

2021-09-26
german potatoe traditional vegetables vegetarian

Ingredients (For 8 pancakes)

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 3 eggs
  • fresh parsley
  • fresh chives
  • chestnut
  • flour to bind, around 130gr

Optional

  • Clove of garlic, finely diced
  • Bacon cubes

To Serve

  • Applesauce

Instructions

  1. First peel potatoes and onion, then finely grate them
  2. Squeeze mass to make it a bit dryer
  3. Give eggs, parsley, chives, chestnut, salt & pepper to mass, mix it
  4. Give oil or some fat to a pan, bake individually
